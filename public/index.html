Key Components

Cannabidiol

Established plant medicine. Our bodies are naturally receptive to CBD, making it effective for pain relief, trauma resilience, cancer prevention, and countless other uses.

Probiotics

Living microorganisms that improve digestion, promote a healthy gut biome (your ecosystem of intestinal bacteria), strengthen your immune system and improve mental health.

Turmeric

Turmeric is an anti-inflammatory, anti-viral, and antioxidant root that is linked to fighting depression, diabetes, joint pain and improve liver function. Combine with CBD for superpowers.

Spirulina

Spirulina is a blue-green algae with exceptional protein and vitamin content. Termed a superfood, it can lower cholesterol, boost metabolism, and aid in weight management.
